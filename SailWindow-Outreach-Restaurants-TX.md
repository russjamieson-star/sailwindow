# SailWindow — Marina-Adjacent Restaurant Outreach (6 New TX Stations)
*Compiled July 9, 2026 from public web listings — re-verify address/phone/ownership before sending. Internal use.*

## The pitch angle

Same as the FL batch: SailWindow's "ashore" panel shows sailors nearby dining once they pick a marina/station — these restaurants already get organic visibility to every boater checking conditions before heading out. The ask: confirm their listing is accurate (hours, phone, dockage), and consider a small reciprocal push (QR code at the host stand, mention in their newsletter) in exchange for a featured/verified badge in the app. Low-pressure, mutual-benefit framing — not a sales pitch.
