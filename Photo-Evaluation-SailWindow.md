## Bottom line (original batch 1)

You have a genuinely usable marketing set — about **6–7 hero-grade frames and ~9 solid gallery/social supports**, plus a tail of candid personal snapshots. The biggest strength is **authenticity**: these are real, older, recreational Gulf Coast sailors on a classic bay boat — exactly the Navy YC / PYC / Grand Lagoon member who needs to see themselves in the brand. Nothing here looks like the glossy offshore "big boys," which keeps you on the right side of your positioning rule.

The main gaps: only one frame shows a phone in use (no crisp screen-of-the-app shot), and there's no bright **morning** establishing shot — the thing you literally sell ("sells to the morning").
