SailWindow Restaurant Partner Program

SailWindow is a coastal sailing app used by active mariners from Texas to the Carolinas. When a subscriber docks at a nearby marina and opens the app, your restaurant appears as a featured Partner — with your agreed discount displayed prominently at the top of their dining list.

SailWindow pre-loads its marina and restaurant database to each subscriber's device. By the time a sailor ties off, your listing and discount offer are already on their screen — no waiting for data to load at the dock. The decision of where to eat is made before they step off the boat.

This document outlines the partnership between SailWindow LLC and your restaurant, and serves as the operational reference for your staff. Please retain it on-site.

For Staff

How to handle a redemption

1
Subscriber shows the app
A sailor presents their phone displaying the SailWindow Partner Discount Screen. The screen has a dark background, teal branding, your restaurant's name, and a dashed "Show this to your server" panel (illustrated below).
2
Confirm the screen
Verify that the screen shows your restaurant's name and the agreed offer. No code, printed voucher, or verbal password is required. A screenshot of the screen is also acceptable — sailors occasionally dock in areas with limited cell service.
3
Apply the discount at checkout
Apply the offer to the check. Nothing needs to be reported back to SailWindow.

Program Terms

Updates and changes

What SailWindow provides

Featured placement in the app for the marina(s) associated with your restaurant — your name, address, phone, rating, hours, and your agreed discount offer — at no cost to you.

Updates to the program

SailWindow may update program terms, the app's design, or the redemption process. Your restaurant will be notified in advance of any change that affects how staff handle a redemption.

To request a change to your listing (hours, phone, offer text) or to pause or end the partnership, contact SailWindow at ahoy@sailwindow.com or 404-585-1950. Changes take effect once confirmed by SailWindow in writing.

Either party may end the partnership at any time

No penalties apply. SailWindow will remove the listing from the app within 48 hours of a written request.
