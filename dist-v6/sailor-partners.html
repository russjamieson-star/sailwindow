What the Partner Program Is

Subscriber discounts at dockside restaurants

SailWindow has partnered with select restaurants near participating marinas. Each Partner has agreed to offer SailWindow subscribers an exclusive discount — displayed directly in the app when you dock.

Partners are vetted by SailWindow and featured prominently in the Ashore panel, above general dining listings. Every Partner has agreed to the offer you see — there is nothing to negotiate at the table.

The discount is confirmed before you walk in. What's shown in the app is exactly what the restaurant has agreed to. Present the screen, receive the discount.

How to Use It

Three steps from dock to table

1
Dock and open SailWindow
Tap the Ashore tab. Partner restaurants appear at the top of your marina's dining list, marked with a tag icon and the word PARTNER.
2
Tap the Partner listing
Your Partner Discount Screen opens — showing the restaurant's name, your specific offer, and a "Show this to your server" panel. Keep this screen open when you arrive.
3
Show the screen to your server
Mention SailWindow when you arrive or present the screen to your server. The discount will be applied to your check — no coupon code or printed voucher needed.

Common Questions

What to know before you go

Does every restaurant in the Ashore list offer a discount?
No. Only restaurants labeled PARTNER have agreed to a subscriber discount. The full Ashore list includes all nearby dining — Partners are featured at the top.
Do I need cell service at the dock?
No. Once you've opened the Partner Discount Screen, you can take a screenshot. A screenshot is acceptable — Partners know this.
What if the restaurant is unfamiliar with the program?
Ask for a manager and show them this page or the app screen. If the issue persists, contact SailWindow at 404-585-1950 and we will follow up with the restaurant directly.
Does the discount apply to the whole table?
Yes — the offer applies to your dining party. Refer to the specific offer text in the app for any limits.
Can I suggest a restaurant for the program?
Absolutely. Email ahoy@sailwindow.com with the restaurant name and marina. We actively expand the Partner network based on subscriber feedback.
