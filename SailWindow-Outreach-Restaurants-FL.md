## The pitch angle

SailWindow's "ashore" panel shows sailors nearby dining once they pick a marina/station — these restaurants already get organic visibility to every boater checking conditions before heading out. The ask: confirm their listing is accurate (hours, phone, dockage), and consider a small reciprocal push (QR code at the host stand, mention in their newsletter) in exchange for a featured/verified badge in the app. Low-pressure, mutual-benefit framing — not a sales pitch.

## Compliance footnote (commercial email)

CAN-SPAM basics: truthful subject line, physical business address in the footer, a working opt-out honored promptly. Template covers all three — keep the last two lines in every send.
