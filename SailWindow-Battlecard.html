It knows your comfort range and trip length, scores every daylight window 0–10, hands you a departure time, and tracks which Gulf marinas are actually operational after the storms. Windy gives everyone the same map. SailWindow gives you an answer — for the price of a burgee."

Rapid-fire rebuttals

"It's all just NOAA data." So is everyone's. NOAA's Small Craft Advisory is one-size-fits-all; SailWindow is your-size. The product is the judgment, not the data.
"Windy is free." A map should be free. An answer is worth $5.99 — less than one gallon of diesel.
"I just look at the sky." The sky at 9 AM doesn't show the 2 PM gust line or the ebb at the pass. The score does.
